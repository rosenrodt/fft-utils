  • Algorithm
    1. Perform NxN_xNx​ radix-NyN_yNy​ FFTs along the high dimension NyN_yNy​
    2. Multiply by twiddle factor WNxNynxkyW^{n_x k_y}_{N_x N_y}WNx​Ny​nx​ky​​
    3. Perform NyN_yNy​ radix-NxN_xNx​ FFTs along the low dimension NxN_xNx​
    4. Read the resulting data transposed
